# 📖 Project Description

EcoSort AI is an intelligent waste segregation and recycling assistant designed to promote sustainable waste management practices.

Many individuals struggle to identify the correct disposal methods for different types of waste, resulting in improper segregation, increased landfill accumulation, and environmental pollution.

EcoSort AI leverages Artificial Intelligence to classify waste items, provide disposal recommendations, explain environmental impacts, and encourage sustainable behavior through personalized sustainability insights.

The system accepts both text-based waste descriptions and image uploads, making waste identification simple and accessible for everyone.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Improper waste disposal remains a significant environmental challenge across households, educational institutions, and communities.

Many people are unaware of:

- Correct waste segregation methods
- Recycling opportunities
- Environmental impacts of waste
- Sustainable disposal practices

This lack of awareness often leads to:

- Increased landfill waste
- Pollution of water bodies
- Resource wastage
- Reduced recycling efficiency
- Environmental degradation

An intelligent AI-driven assistant can help users make informed decisions regarding waste management and promote sustainable consumption practices.

---

# 💡 Solution Overview

EcoSort AI provides an AI-powered platform that:

### Text-Based Classification

Users can enter any waste item such as:

- Plastic Bottle
- Coffee Cup
- Newspaper
- Old Charger
- Battery
- Food Waste

The system automatically:

- Classifies the waste
- Identifies disposal methods
- Explains environmental impact
- Generates sustainability recommendations

### Image-Based Waste Recognition

Users can upload waste images.

The AI model:

- Detects the waste item
- Identifies the category
- Suggests disposal methods

### Sustainability Insights

The system generates:

- Environmental impact analysis
- Recycling tips
- Sustainability recommendations
- Sustainability scores
